About Us

C & C International Human Resource Management Services (C & C International) was established on 24 October 2019. It is the newest business venture set up by Atty. Imelda Barcelona, the CEO of AGILE Maritime Resources Inc. and Lubeca Protection & Indemnity.

Our core business is preparing Filipino professionals to be job-ready to work overseas. Proficiency in the language of the host country is the key requirement to secure a work visa.

We are proud of our highly professional language team. Our teachers are certified by the highest authority in German language , culture and government and hold degrees in European languages from top universities in the Philippines and in Germany.

>Our hardworking team will develop, train and sharpen your language skills to make sure that you will pass the language proficiency examinations.

In C & C International , we promise to be the bridge that will connect you to your dream.

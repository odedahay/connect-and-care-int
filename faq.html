FAQ about C&C

How does the program work?

German lessons are conducted online. We will ask you to submit a detailed CV and a cover letter containing the info set out in question no. 2. You will receive a prompt reply containing available payment schemes. We will email you confirmation of receipt of payment and your class schedule.

What to write on the cover letter of your Cover Letter

It is important for us to know the reasons why you want to learn the German language. Listed below are questions to guide you in composing your cover letter.

  1. Brief self-introduction
  2. What is your motivation for wanting to migrate to Germany? For work? To join a family member?
  3. If for work , what are your expectations of the job in Germany?
  4. Where or from whom did you learn about our programme?

Please submit your cover letter and CV to media@cnc-international.com. Expect a prompt reply Monday- Friday , 8am to 5pm

Im a Nurse/Engineer and would like know if there are employment opportunities available after I completed the language levels and getting my proficiency certificates?

Work opportunities especially for nurses abound in Germany. The German government opened its doors for professionals who are interested to work in Germany. Proficiency in the German language is the key that will open the door of Germany to you.

Can the program accommodate my schedule if I’m still employed?

Yes. Once you confirm your participation in the programme, we will try to accommodate your preferred time of the day.

Can I still participate in the programme even if I’m overseas ?

Yes. We welcome Filipino overseas workers to our programme. The big plus of online learning is it is very flexible. We will be happy to adjust our class schedules to adapt to the student’s time zone

Do you provide learning materials?

Yes, we will provide you with learning materials before the start of the course.

Is my instructor a Filipino or German national?

We have both. Our team is composed of experienced and highly qualified teachers with university degrees in German and other European languages. Our teachers are regular commuters to Germany - they have adapted well to the German way of life.

What are the things I need to prepare before the start of the course?

The participants in our Online German Lessons Programme will be needing the following, viz:

  1. Laptop with Mircosoft Team App
  2. Reliable Internet connection
  3. Burning desire to learn the German language - the key that will open the door to big and better opportunities in the land of poets and thinkers.
  4. Commitment to pursue and finish the entire programme

Do you have one on one session?

For now, we only accommodate a class with at least 5-7 students.

Are you also a recruitment agency?

No. C&C International is a language training provider. Our expertise is to prepare professionals to be job-ready for Germany. We have a partner agency licensed to engage in Landbased recruitment .

Do you accept other professionals or students who are interested to learn German language?

Yes, we welcome any one who is interested to learn the German language.
